Enable app embed

App embed is mandatory for review widget to display on the store. It is also essential to display collected reviews
properly. Click on the below-given button and click Save to activate it.

Proviews Widget Integration
Step 1
Open Shopify backend and navigation to Online>>Themes>>Customize
Step 2
From the dropdown select the product template where you’re willing to insert the Proviews widget
Step 3
From the left panel, navigate to the Add section and select Proviews widget
Step 4
Change the position of the widget and then click on Save
Make sure to click on “save” after each change
